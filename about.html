ABOUT US

Deccan Chronicle Holdings Limited (DCHL) publishes the largest circulated English newspaper in South India -- Deccan Chronicle, bringing you every day the most comprehensive bouquet of news and analysis. Its fearless and unbiased journalism over 75 years has won the Deccan Chronicle a mature and dedicated readership from all over the world. And, it is growing.

The Deccan Chronicle has a circulation of over 1.45 million copies each day across Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka and Kerala. There are eight editions in Andhra Pradesh and Telangana -- Hyderabad, Vijayawada, Rajahmundry, Vishakapatnam, Anantapur, Karimnagar, Nellore and Coimbatore. The paper also has a robust presence in Chennai, Bengaluru and Kochi.

DCHL also publishes The Asian Age, an English daily with editions in Mumbai, Delhi, Kolkata, and London. The group's financial daily is the Financial Chronicle which publishes from Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Bangalore and Chennai. Also in the stable is the very popular Telugu paper Andhra Bhoomi.

DCHL also operates Odyssey -- a neighbourhood leisure store that keeps close to its heart the aspirational needs of the consumer and offers a whole gamut of lifestyle products such as books, CDs, stationery and gifts. There are several stores in Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Karnataka, Maharashtra and the National Capital Region.
